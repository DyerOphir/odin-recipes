Lasanga Recipe

Description

This dish is an incredibly cheesy dish that involves a lot of MEAT.

This dish also came from my girlfriends Italian heritage lmao.

Ingredients:

  • Meat: This super meaty lasanga has sweet Italian sasuage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and Garlic: An onion and two gloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato Products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle weetness to enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: This lasanga recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasanga noodles: Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheese: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

Layering the Lasagna:

  1. Meat Sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta Mixture
  4. Mozarella Slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining parmesan.
